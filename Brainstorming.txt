PROBLEM- Many people who watch or read East Asian dramas, movies or webtoon or listen to songs cannot find all of these in one website or app.
Some people try watching on unsafe websites or apps which have a lot of inappropriate ads. On top of that, some apps also require 
paid subscriptions to be able to watch dramas or movies.

SOLUTION- Creating a one-stop platform for all things related to asian culture like Music, Movies, dramas, news, webtoons etc makes it easier 
for people to entertain themselves with Asian entertainment.
